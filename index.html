// How It Works

The Problem Is In
Your Water Meter.

Your water meter measures volume — not just water. It can't distinguish between water and the air that enters the line as pressure drops. You're billed for both. The Smart Valve fixes that.

01
💧

Air Enters Your Line

When you demand water, pressure drops. As pressure drops, air expands and enters the water volume passing through your meter. Your meter measures it as water.

02
⚙️

The Smart Valve Maintains Pressure

The patented Variable Flow Control device installs upstream of your meter, maintaining higher pressure at the meter. Air can't expand — so it doesn't get measured.

03
📊

Your Bill Drops Immediately

From the first billing cycle after installation, you pay only for water you actually use. Average reduction: 15–28% on combined water and sewer charges.

04
🔧

Externally Adjustable — Forever

The 2nd and 3rd generation Smart Valves are externally adjustable for maximum performance. Fine-tune to your facility, adapt to future system changes — no shutdowns, ever.
